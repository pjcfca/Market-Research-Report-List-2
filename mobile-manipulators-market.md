<p><p>Mobile manipulators are robotic systems equipped with both a mobile base and an articulated arm for performing tasks in various environments. The omnidirectional market includes manipulators with bases capable of moving in any direction, providing increased flexibility and maneuverability. Non-omnidirectional manipulators have bases with limited mobility, restricting their movement to certain directions. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements, with omnidirectional manipulators excelling in complex, crowded spaces, while non-omnidirectional manipulators may be more cost-effective and simpler to operate.</p></p>
