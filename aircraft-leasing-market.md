<p><strong>The Aircraft Leasing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Leasing</li><li>Wet Leasing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft leasing market consists of two main types: Dry Leasing and Wet Leasing. Dry Leasing involves leasing an aircraft without crew, maintenance, insurance, and other operational services. The lessee is responsible for these aspects. On the other hand, Wet Leasing involves leasing an aircraft with crew, maintenance, insurance, and other operational services included. This allows the lessee to use the aircraft for a specific period without having to worry about the operational details. Both types offer flexibility and cost-saving benefits to airlines and other operators.</p></p>
