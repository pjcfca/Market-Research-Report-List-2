<p><p>Fiber Optic Multiplexers are used to combine multiple signals onto a single fiber optic cable. There are two main types of markets for Fiber Optic Multiplexers - Frequency Division Multiplexing (FDM) and Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM). FDM involves dividing the available bandwidth into separate frequency channels for different signals, while WDM uses different wavelengths of light to separate signals. Both FDM and WDM markets are essential for increasing the efficiency and capacity of fiber optic networks by allowing multiple signals to be transmitted simultaneously.</p></p>
