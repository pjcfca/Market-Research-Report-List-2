<p><ul><li>Immersion Heated Circulating Bash</li><li>Open Heated Circulating Bash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heated circulating baths come in two main market types: immersion heated circulating baths and open heated circulating baths. Immersion heated circulating baths are designed with a heating element submerged in the bath fluid, providing uniform and consistent heating. Open heated circulating baths, on the other hand, rely on an external circulator to heat the bath fluid, allowing for greater flexibility in temperature control. Both types of heated circulating baths are used in various industries such as research, healthcare, and manufacturing for tasks like sample preparation and temperature calibration.</p></p>
