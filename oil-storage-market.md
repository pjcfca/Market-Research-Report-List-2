<p><p>Oil storage tanks come in various types such as Open Top Tanks, Fixed Roof Tanks, Floating Roof Tanks, and others. Open Top Tanks are used for storing crude oil, while Fixed Roof Tanks are commonly used for storing gasoline and other volatile liquids. Floating Roof Tanks are designed to reduce evaporation losses and are suitable for storing large quantities of oil. Other types of tanks include Cone Roof Tanks, Dome Roof Tanks, and External Floating Roof Tanks which have specific applications based on factors like cost, space restrictions, and environmental regulations.</p></p>
