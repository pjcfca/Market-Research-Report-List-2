<p><p>Linear motion guide rails are categorized into different types based on the type of bearings they use. Ball guide rails use small steel balls to reduce friction and allow smooth linear movement. Roller guide rails use cylindrical rollers for higher load capacity and durability. Needle guide rails use needle bearings for precise movement in tight spaces. Other types of guide rails include air bearings, magnetic bearings, and hybrid bearings. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
