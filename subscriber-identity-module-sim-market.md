<p><p>The Subscriber Identity Module (SIM) card comes in four different market types: Full-size (FF), Mini-SIM (2FF), Micro-SIM (3FF), and Nano-SIM (4FF). The Full-size (FF) SIM card was the original standard, followed by the smaller Mini-SIM (2FF), then the even smaller Micro-SIM (3FF), and the smallest Nano-SIM (4FF). These different sizes allow SIM cards to fit into various devices, from older phones that use Full-size SIM cards to newer phones and devices that require Nano-SIM cards.</p></p>
