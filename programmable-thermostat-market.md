<p><p>The programmable thermostat market can be divided into different types based on the technology used for connectivity. WiFi thermostats enable remote access and control through a wireless network connection. ZigBee thermostats use a low-power wireless technology for communication within a home automation network. Bluetooth thermostats allow for short-range wireless communication with mobile devices. Other types of programmable thermostats may utilize different connectivity options such as Z-Wave or proprietary protocols. Each type offers varying levels of convenience and functionality for users.</p></p>
