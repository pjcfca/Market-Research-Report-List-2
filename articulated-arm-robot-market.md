<p><strong>The Articulated Arm Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transversely Articulated</li><li>Longitudinal Articulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Articulated arm robots come in two main types: transversely articulated and longitudinally articulated. Transversely articulated robots have joints that allow for movement across a horizontal plane, making them ideal for tasks that involve reaching and navigating in different directions. On the other hand, longitudinally articulated robots have joints that allow for movement along a vertical plane, making them more suitable for tasks that require precision and accuracy in a linear direction. Both types of robots offer unique capabilities and are designed to meet specific application requirements.</p></p>
