<p><ul><li>Single-pole Rocker Switch</li><li>Multipole Rocker Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rocker switches are commonly categorized into two main types: single-pole and multipole. Single-pole rocker switches connect or disconnect the circuit from a single conductor, making them suitable for basic on/off functions. Multipole rocker switches, on the other hand, have multiple poles that allow for more complex switching operations, such as controlling different circuits simultaneously. Both types of switches are widely used in electrical and electronic devices for their easy operation and reliability.</p></p>
