<p><p>Workflow Management Systems can be classified into two main market types: Cloud-based and On-Premises Model. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage workflows through the internet. This model offers flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. On the other hand, On-Premises Model involves hosting the system on the organization's own servers, providing more control over data security and customization. However, this model requires higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance costs. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the market.</p></p>
