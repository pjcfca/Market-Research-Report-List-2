<p><ul><li>Multi-port Robotic System</li><li>Single-port Robotic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgery Robotic Market consists of two main types: multi-port robotic systems and single-port robotic systems. Multi-port robotic systems utilize multiple robotic arms to perform various surgical tasks simultaneously, offering greater flexibility and precision. On the other hand, single-port robotic systems use a single access point for all surgical instruments, making them more compact and minimally invasive. Both types of systems are revolutionizing the field of surgery by enhancing surgical capabilities and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
