<p><p>Electric Food Dehydrators come in three main types: Conduction Type, Convection Type, and Radiation Type. The Conduction Type dehydrators use direct heat transfer to remove moisture from food. The Convection Type dehydrators circulate hot air around the food to dry it out. The Radiation Type dehydrators use infrared radiation to remove moisture from the food. Each type offers different advantages and disadvantages, catering to different preferences and needs of consumers in the market.</p></p>
