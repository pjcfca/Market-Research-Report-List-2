<p><p>Portable Gas Chromatograph is a compact analytical instrument used to separate and analyze compounds in a gaseous sample. Gas-liquid Chromatograph utilizes a liquid stationary phase, typically coated on a solid support material, to separate compounds based on their interactions with the stationary phase. Gas-solid Chromatograph uses a solid stationary phase to separate compounds based on their interactions with the solid surface. Both types of portable gas chromatographs are commonly used in fields such as environmental monitoring, food safety, and pharmaceutical analysis.</p></p>
