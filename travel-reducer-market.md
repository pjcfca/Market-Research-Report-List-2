<p><strong>The Travel Reducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flange Mounted</li><li>Shaft Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Travel reducers are used in machinery to reduce speed and increase torque for various applications. There are two main types of travel reducers - flange mounted and shaft mounted. Flange mounted travel reducers are directly mounted onto the machinery using flanges, providing a compact and easy installation option. On the other hand, shaft mounted travel reducers are mounted directly onto the shaft of the machinery, offering a more versatile and adaptable solution for different types of machinery and applications. Both types of travel reducers serve the purpose of reducing speed and increasing torque efficiently.</p></p>
