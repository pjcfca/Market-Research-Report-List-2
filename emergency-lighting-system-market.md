<p><strong>The Emergency Lighting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Contained Power System</li><li>Central Power System</li><li>Hybrid Power System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emergency Lighting System Market offers three main types of systems: self-contained power system, central power system, and hybrid power system. Self-contained power system consists of battery-powered lighting units that operate independently from the building's main power supply. Central power system connects all emergency lights to a central battery backup, ensuring all lights are powered from one common source. Hybrid power system combines elements of both self-contained and central systems, providing a flexible and reliable emergency lighting solution for various applications.</p></p>
