Transmitters are responsible for encoding and transmitting data through optical signals, while Receivers detect and decode these signals. Modulators and Demodulators are used for signal modulation and demodulation, respectively. Other components in the market include amplifiers, filters, switches, and multiplexers that play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and performance of free-space optical communication systems.</p></p>
