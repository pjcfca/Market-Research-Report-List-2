<p><ul><li>General Capacitive Switch</li><li>Hybrid Capacitive Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive switches can be broadly classified into two types - General Capacitive Switch and Hybrid Capacitive Switch. General capacitive switches use a touch-sensitive technology to detect changes in capacitance when a finger or stylus comes close to the switch. On the other hand, hybrid capacitive switches combine capacitive technology with other sensing technologies such as resistive or piezoelectric sensors to enhance the overall performance and functionality of the switch. Both types offer reliable and responsive touch controls for various electronic devices.</p></p>
