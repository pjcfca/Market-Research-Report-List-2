<p><strong>The Double Column Machining Center Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Beam Type</li><li>Moving Beam Type</li><li>Moving Column Type</li><li>Crane Type</li><li>Compound Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed Beam Type double column machining centers have a stationary beam and moving table. Moving Beam Type features a moving beam and stationary table. Moving Column Type has a moving column and fixed table. Crane Type machining centers have a crane-like structure for heavy-duty applications. Compound Type combines vertical and horizontal machining capabilities in one machine. Each type caters to specific machining needs, offering distinct advantages in terms of rigidity, precision, versatility, and load-bearing capacity. The variety of options available in the market allows for customization based on individual requirements.</p></p>
