<p><p>Manure scraper market types include Flap Scraper, V-Form Scraper, U-Form Scraper, and Others. Flap scrapers are designed with a hinged flap that moves along the ground to push manure towards a collection area. V-Form scrapers consist of two angled blades that move in a V-shaped pattern to gather manure. U-Form scrapers have a U-shaped design that collects manure as they move along. Other types of scrapers may include unique designs or variations of the aforementioned types to suit specific needs in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
