<p><ul><li>Robotic Arms</li><li>Track Robots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab robotics market is segmented into two major types: Robotic Arms and Track Robots. Robotic Arms are flexible and can perform various tasks such as pipetting, sample handling, and plate movement. They are widely used in laboratory settings for automation of repetitive tasks. Track Robots, on the other hand, are mounted on a track system and are used for transporting samples and labware between different instruments and workstations. Both types of robots are crucial in increasing efficiency and productivity in laboratory workflows.</p></p>
