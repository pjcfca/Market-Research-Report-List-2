<p><p>The Electromagnetic Pump for Liquid Metal market consists of two main types: Conduction Pump and Induction Pump. Conduction Pump utilizes the flow of electric current through a conductive liquid metal to generate a magnetic field and propel the liquid metal forward. On the other hand, Induction Pump uses electromagnetic induction to create a rotating magnetic field, which in turn moves the liquid metal. Both pump types are widely used in industries such as metal casting, nuclear power, and material processing for their efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
