<p><p>Trailer locks are essential for securing trailers and preventing theft. Two common types of trailer locks are latch locks and surround locks. Latch locks securely fasten onto the trailer's latch, preventing it from being opened. Surround locks, on the other hand, encase the entire trailer hitch, making it impossible for the hitch to be accessed and removed. Both latch locks and surround locks provide effective security measures for trailers, ensuring that valuable cargo or equipment is protected from theft.</p></p>
