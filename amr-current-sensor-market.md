<p><ul><li>Closed Loop AMR Current Sensor</li><li>Open Loop AMR Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AMR current sensors are divided into two main types: Closed Loop and Open Loop. Closed Loop AMR current sensors provide feedback to the control system, offering high accuracy and stability. Open Loop AMR current sensors do not require feedback, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Closed Loop sensors are ideal for precise measurements in critical applications, while Open Loop sensors are suitable for general-purpose usage where high accuracy is not essential. Both types cater to different market needs and offer unique benefits based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
