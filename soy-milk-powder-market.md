<p><p>Soy milk powder comes in two main market types: GMO and non-GMO. Non-GMO soy milk powder, on the other hand, is made from soybeans that have not been genetically altered. Consumers who prefer to avoid GMOs may choose non-GMO soy milk powder for health or ethical reasons, while those less concerned may opt for the more readily available GMO variety.</p></p>
