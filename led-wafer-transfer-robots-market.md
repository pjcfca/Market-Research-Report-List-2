<p><p>LED wafer transfer robots play a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing processes. There are two main types of wafer transfer robots: atmospheric and vacuum. Atmospheric wafer transfer robots operate in normal air pressure conditions, while vacuum wafer transfer robots function in low-pressure environments to prevent contamination. Atmospheric robots are typically used for general wafer handling tasks, while vacuum robots are preferred for delicate operations where cleanliness is critical. Both types play a key role in ensuring the efficient and precise transfer of wafers during LED production.</p></p>
