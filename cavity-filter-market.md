<p><p>A cavity filter is a type of filter that operates on the principle of electromagnetic resonance within a cavity resonator. It is commonly used in radio frequency (RF) applications to selectively pass or attenuate signals within a specific frequency range. Cavity filters offer high Q factors, excellent selectivity, and low insertion loss, making them ideal for various wireless communication systems, radar systems, and broadcast applications.</p><p>The global cavity filter market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, driven by increasing demand for wireless communication technologies and advancements in the telecommunications sector.
