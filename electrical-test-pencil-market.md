<p><p>Electrical test pencils are commonly divided into two types: Contact Type Test Pencils and Induction Type Test Pencils. Contact Type Test Pencils rely on physical contact with a conductor to detect the presence of voltage, while Induction Type Test Pencils can detect voltage without coming into direct contact with the conductor. Both types are used by electricians and technicians to safely test for the presence of voltage in electrical circuits. The market for these test pencils caters to professionals in various industries where electrical testing is a crucial part of their work.</p></p>
