<p><ul><li>Limit Switch</li><li>Proximity switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Position switches are vital components in industrial automation and machinery. The market types include limit switches and proximity switches. Limit switches are mechanical devices that detect the presence or absence of an object by physically contacting it. Proximity switches, on the other hand, use electromagnetic fields or other sensing technologies to detect objects without physical contact. Both types of switches offer reliability and durability in various industrial applications, ensuring smooth and efficient operation of machines and equipment.</p></p>
