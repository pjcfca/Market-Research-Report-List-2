<p><p>Visible Light Communications (VLC) market consists of two main types – Access Points and Station Points. Access Points are devices that emit visible light signals to transmit data to connected devices, serving as a central hub in the network. Station Points, on the other hand, are devices that receive these light signals and can communicate with the Access Points. Access Points are commonly used in public spaces like airports or malls, while Station Points are typically found in homes or offices for seamless data transmission.</p></p>
