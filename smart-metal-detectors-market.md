<p><p>Smart metal detectors come in various types including rectangular aperture metal detectors, gravity fall metal detectors, pipeline liquid, paste and slurry metal detectors, and metal detectors with conveyor. Rectangular aperture metal detectors are used for inspecting products on a conveyor belt, while gravity fall metal detectors are ideal for free-falling applications. Pipeline liquid, paste and slurry metal detectors are designed for detecting metal contamination in flowing products. Metal detectors with conveyor combine the features of conveyor systems with metal detector technology for efficient product inspection.</p></p>
