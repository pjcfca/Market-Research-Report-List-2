<p><p>Inflight Internet Systems can be categorized into three main types: Air-To-Ground (ATG) systems, Ka Band Satellite systems, and Ku Band Satellite systems. ATG systems use ground-based stations to provide internet connectivity to aircrafts. Ka Band Satellite systems utilize high frequency satellites for faster and more efficient internet access while Ku Band Satellite systems use lower frequency satellites for broader coverage and reliable connectivity. Each system type offers different benefits and options for airlines looking to provide internet services to passengers during flight.</p></p>
