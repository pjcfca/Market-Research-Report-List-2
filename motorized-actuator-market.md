<p><p>Motorized actuators with stepper motors are widely used for applications that require precise positioning and high resolution. They operate by converting electrical pulses into mechanical motion in fixed increments. On the other hand, actuators with servo motors are preferred for applications that require high speed and smooth operation. They provide accurate and fast positioning by continuously receiving feedback from a position sensor. Both types of actuators have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
