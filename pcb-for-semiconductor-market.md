<p><p>PCBs are crucial components in the semiconductor market, with two main types: Rigid PCB and Flexible PCB. Rigid PCBs are traditional circuit boards made of solid substrate materials, suitable for applications requiring stability and durability. In contrast, Flexible PCBs are made of flexible substrates, allowing them to be bent or twisted, ideal for applications with limited space or requiring unique shapes. Both types cater to different market needs, providing essential support for the growth of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
