<p><p>Polymer resettable fuses are available in two main market types: in-line and surface mount. In-line resettable fuses are typically used in applications where space may be limited, and are often installed directly into the circuit. Surface mount resettable fuses are designed to be mounted on the surface of a printed circuit board, making them easier to install and replace. Both types of fuses provide protection against overcurrent conditions by automatically resetting once the fault is cleared, making them a reliable choice for a variety of electronic devices.</p></p>
