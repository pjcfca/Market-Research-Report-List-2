<p><p>Wavefront sensors are utilized in various applications such as astronomy, medical imaging, and microscopy. There are several types of wavefront sensors including Shack-Hartmann, Wavefront Curvature, Pyramid, Common Path Interferometer, and Multilateral Shearing Interferometer. Shack-Hartmann sensors use an array of lenslets to measure wavefront distortion, Wavefront Curvature sensors use a reflective or refractive surface to measure wavefront phase changes, Pyramid sensors work by splitting light into four separate beams, Common Path Interferometers utilize interference patterns, and Multilateral Shearing Interferometers measure wavefront gradients using multiple shearing plates.</p></p>
