<p><p>The Auto Drive System Market can be categorized into three main types: Perception System, Decision-Making System, and Control Execution System. The Perception System involves sensors and cameras that collect data from the environment to understand the surroundings. The Decision-Making System processes this data and determines the appropriate action to take. Finally, the Control Execution System translates these decisions into physical movements, such as steering the vehicle or adjusting speed. These three components work together seamlessly to enable autonomous driving capabilities.</p></p>
