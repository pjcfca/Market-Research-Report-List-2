<p><p>Optoelectric Nuclear Battery Market can be classified into two types: Thermal Conversion Type and No-Thermal Conversion Type. </p><p>Thermal Conversion Type relies on converting the heat generated from the nuclear reaction into electricity, using thermoelectric or thermophotovoltaic processes. </p><p>On the other hand, No-Thermal Conversion Type utilizes photonuclear reactions to directly convert nuclear energy into electricity without generating heat. This type of battery is more efficient and compact compared to the thermal conversion type, making it a promising technology for the future.</p></p>
