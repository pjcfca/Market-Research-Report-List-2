<p><p>Integrated Circuit Packaging Market includes various types such as Metal, Ceramics, and Glass packaging. Metal packaging typically offers good thermal conductivity and flexibility. Ceramics packaging provides excellent thermal stability and resistance to harsh environments. Glass packaging, on the other hand, offers high electrical insulation and low coefficient of thermal expansion. Each type of packaging material has its unique characteristics and is used in different applications based on factors like performance requirements, cost-effectiveness, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
