<p><p>Compressor blades are a crucial component in jet engines and are divided into high-pressure and low-pressure types. High-pressure compressor blades are located near the front of the engine and handle the highest air pressure, while low-pressure compressor blades are located further back and handle lower pressure levels. Both types play a vital role in compressing air within the engine, but high-pressure blades are designed to withstand greater forces and temperatures compared to low-pressure blades.</p></p>
