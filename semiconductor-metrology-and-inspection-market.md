Wafer Inspection Systems are used to detect defects on the surface of semiconductor wafers during the production process. Mask Inspection Systems are used to ensure the accuracy and quality of photomasks, which are essential for patterning features onto wafers. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor devices, ultimately impacting the performance of electronic products.</p></p>
