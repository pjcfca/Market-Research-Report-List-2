<p><p>Piezoceramic components are essential in various industrial applications for precise control and manipulation of mechanical movements. PZT-based components are known for their high dielectric constants and piezoelectric properties, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. PMN-based components offer higher sensitivity and lower hysteresis, making them ideal for more demanding industrial environments. Lastly, other types of piezoceramic components cater to specific niche applications within the industrial sector.</p></p>
