<p><strong>The Hydro Turbine Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Hydro Turbine Generator</li><li>Reaction Hydro Turbine Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydro Turbine Generators are classified into two types based on their operating principle: Impulse and Reaction turbines. Impulse turbines use the kinetic energy of water to generate electricity, while Reaction turbines utilize both the kinetic and potential energy of water. Impulse turbines are suitable for high head, low flow applications, while Reaction turbines are ideal for low head, high flow conditions. Both types of turbines play a crucial role in harnessing hydropower for generating electricity efficiently and sustainably.</p></p>
