<p><strong>The Electronic Tongue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Type</li><li>Amperometric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Potentiometric type electronic tongue market involves sensors that measure voltage changes due to chemical reactions between the sample and the electrode. This type is commonly used due to its simplicity and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, the amperometric type electronic tongue market measures electric current produced by the oxidation or reduction of molecules in the sample. This type offers high sensitivity and selectivity, making it ideal for various applications in fields like food and beverage analysis.</p></p>
